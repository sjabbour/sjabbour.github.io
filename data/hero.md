I am an incoming Assistant Professor of Applied AI at [Chicago Booth](https://www.chicagobooth.edu/), starting July 2026. 

I completed my PhD in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Michigan, where I was advised by Professors [Jenna Wiens](https://web.eecs.umich.edu/~wiensj/) and [David Fouhey](https://cs.nyu.edu/~fouhey/). During my PhD, I also collaborated with [Cliff Wong](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/clwon/) and [Jeya Maria Jose Valanarasu](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/jevalanarasu/) at Microsoft Research, as well as [Mohamed Mostagir](https://michiganross.umich.edu/faculty-research/faculty/mohamed-mostagir) at the University of Michigan.

Prior to my PhD, I completed a dual degree in Computer Science & Engineering (BSE) from the University of Michigan College of Engineering and Business Administration (BBA) from the University of Michigan Ross School of Business.
